Design System / 01

A calm system
for long sessions.

ChatLab is a workbench for testing and tuning conversational agents. The system favors warm neutrals, low-saturation accents, and generous space — built to be lived in for hours without fatigue.

Principles

Three commitments that guide every visual decision.

P/01

Easy on the eyes

Warm off-whites and graphite text — never pure white or black. Saturation stays under 0.10 except in semantic states. The accent is a desaturated sage; it whispers.

P/02

Space, not decoration

Hierarchy comes from spacing, weight, and alignment — not boxes inside boxes. Hairlines only. Every component earns its line.

P/03

Honest density

A pro tool surfaces real data: tokens, latency, model IDs, event logs. Mono carries technical truth; sans carries conversation. They stay in their lanes.

/02

Color

Warm neutrals (h=90) paired with a desaturated sage accent (h=165, c=0.08). Saturation is reserved for meaning.
